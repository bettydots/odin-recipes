Lasagna

Description

Layers of pasta, cheese, bechamel and meat sauce form this decadent layer cake of Italian bliss.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest
